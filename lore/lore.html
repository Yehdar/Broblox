Locked within an abandoned factory, you wake up in a dimly lit room, a countdown timer on the wall. A voice reveals that you're part of a sinister experiment. To survive, solve diabolical puzzles within each room before time runs out. Fail, and face deadly consequences. As you progress, you unearth the tormentor's backstory, a tragic figure seeking to test human limits after his own life shattered. Navigate eerie rooms, solve puzzles under pressure, and unveil the twisted tale of "Escape: Chamber of Trials." Your survival depends on wit, speed, and nerves of steel.
